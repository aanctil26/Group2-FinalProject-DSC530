Fast Food Restaurants Across the United States

This is a list of 10,000 fast-food restaurants provided by Datafiniti's Business Database. The dataset includes the restaurant's address, city, latitude and longitude coordinates, name, and more. Our hope is to be able to map out the areas with the highest concentration of fast food restaurants, to see if it compares to poor health conditions throughout the US. The main variables being used are Latitude, Longitude, #, Zip Code, and Category.

The United States Health Data

The Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation has consolidated a database of Health Information for each county in the United States. The database includes information on life expectancy, prevalence of diabetes, obesity, and alcoholism. For the purpose of this study, we will primarily be looking at Diabetes, Obesity, and Life Expectancy. We will also being using the County variable to help map out health conditions across the United States.
